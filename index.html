About us

We are a family-run business in the heart of Letchworth Garden City, proudly serving our local community since February 2007 when my father Richard opened the doors.

From day one, we've believed in offering a friendly and personal customer service, taking real pride in what we do. Our retail service offers fresh, quality produce at competitive prices, with free delivery to make shopping with us easy and convenient.

We also work closely with local businesses through our catering service, supplying reliable and high-quality produce with the same care, consistency, and a free 6 day delivery our customers have come to trust.
